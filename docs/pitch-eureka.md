# EUREKA — 3-Minute Pitch Script

## Problem (30 seconds)

> **"How long does it take to go from a research idea to a validated breakthrough?"**

Most researchers spend **2 to 4 weeks** doing manual literature review. Reading dozens — sometimes hundreds — of papers, trying to find where the field has gaps, and hoping their idea hasn't already been done.

The problem isn't intelligence. It's **time and noise**. arXiv publishes over 2,000 papers *every day*. No human can keep up. And by the time you're done reading, your window of novelty has already closed.

Academics call this "the literature review trap." Innovators call it "analysis paralysis." The result is the same: **great ideas die before they get a chance.**

---

## Solution (60 seconds)

> **"Eureka is an AI research companion that turns a topic into breakthrough ideas in minutes."**

Eureka runs a **5-agent AI pipeline** that works while you watch:

- **Scout** scans hundreds of arXiv papers and extracts key findings
- **Gap Analyst** maps the white spaces — the areas nobody is talking about yet
- **Innovator** synthesizes novel ideas at the intersection of existing research
- **Critic** evaluates each idea for novelty, feasibility, and coherence
- **Coherence Validator** ensures the full idea set is consistent and well-structured

What comes out isn't just a list of papers. It's a **ranked set of actionable breakthrough ideas** — each with novelty scores, feasibility ratings, citations, and a validation plan.

You get the output of a research team in the time it takes to brew your morning coffee.
